Why web-native matters for research teams

Desktop viewers are powerful, but web-native visualization is easier to distribute across collaborators, reviewers, and stakeholders who need frictionless access to key findings.

  • • Share links instead of binaries
  • • Embed views in internal dashboards
  • • Keep interactive context in publications, docs, and presentations
